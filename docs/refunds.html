Refund Policy

Custom Orders

Because all custom outfits are made specifically for each customer, completed custom orders are generally non-refundable.

Order Cancellation

Orders may be cancelled before production begins. Once production starts, cancellation requests may not be accepted.

Damaged Products

Customers must report damaged or defective products within 48 hours of delivery.

Incorrect Items

If the wrong item is delivered, we will review the issue and provide a suitable solution.

Contact

For refund inquiries, contact customer support.
