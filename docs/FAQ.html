Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Browse our collections, select your preferred style, provide measurements, and complete payment through our secure booking system.

Do you make custom designs?

Yes. We create custom-made outfits tailored to your measurements and preferences.

Do you deliver across Nigeria?

Yes. We offer nationwide delivery across Nigeria.

Do you deliver internationally?

Selected international deliveries may be available. Contact us before placing an order.

How long does production take?

Production usually takes between 5 and 14 business days depending on outfit complexity and order volume.

Can I provide my own fabric?

Yes. Customers may provide their own fabric after discussing requirements with us.

Can I make changes after placing an order?

Changes may only be possible before production begins.

Are payments secure?

Yes. Payments are processed securely through trusted payment providers.

Can I request alterations?

Yes. Minor alterations may be available depending on the situation.

How do I contact customer support?

You can contact us via WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, email, or through our Contact page.
