Shipping Policy

Processing Time

Custom outfit orders are processed within 2-5 business days before production begins.

Production Time

Custom outfits generally require 5-21 business days depending on complexity.

Delivery Areas

We currently deliver throughout Nigeria and selected international destinations.

Shipping Costs

Shipping fees vary depending on location and package size.

Tracking

Where available, customers will receive shipment tracking information.

Delays

Unexpected circumstances such as weather conditions, courier delays, public holidays, or customs procedures may affect delivery times.

Failed Deliveries

Customers are responsible for providing accurate delivery information. Additional charges may apply for re-delivery attempts.
